# AI Email Intelligence for Zoho CRM — User Guide

## 1. Overview

AI Email Intelligence enhances Zoho CRM by analyzing email conversations and providing AI-generated summaries, follow-up actions, and reply drafts directly inside CRM records.

The extension helps users quickly understand customer emails and respond efficiently without leaving Zoho CRM.

---

## 2. Installation

1. Open **Zoho Marketplace**
2. Search for **AI Email Intelligence for Zoho CRM**
3. Click **Install**
4. Grant the required permissions
5. Complete the installation

After installation, the widget becomes available inside CRM records.

---

## 3. Initial Configuration (Required)

Before using the extension, an administrator must configure the AI settings.

### Configure API Settings

1. Open any **Contact record** in Zoho CRM
2. Open the **AI Email Intelligence** panel (right side)
3. Go to **Settings**
4. Enter your AI API Key
5. Select the preferred AI model
6. Click **Save**

The extension is now ready to use.

---

## 4. Using the Extension

### A. Analyze Email

1. Open a Contact record with email conversation
2. Launch the AI Email Intelligence panel
3. Click **Analyze Email**

The extension will generate:
• Email summary
• Key discussion points
• Recommended next steps

---

### B. Generate Follow-up Tasks

1. After analysis completes
2. Click **Generate Tasks**

You can:
• edit task description
• assign owner
• set due date

Click **Create Task** to add it into Zoho CRM.

---

### C. Draft Email Reply

1. Click **Write with AI**
2. Select tone (Professional/Friendly/Casual)
3. Click **Generate Reply**

You may edit the message before sending.
The extension never sends emails automatically.

---

## 5. Customizing Prompts

Administrators can customize prompts used by AI:

• Summary instructions
• Task extraction rules
• Email reply style

This allows organizations to align responses with internal communication standards.

---

## 6. Best Practices

• Ensure emails exist in the record before analysis
• Review generated responses before sending
• Avoid including sensitive regulated data in analysis

---

## 7. Troubleshooting

### Widget not visible

Refresh the CRM page and reopen the record.

### Analyze button disabled

Make sure:
• API key is configured
• Email exists in the record

### No results generated

The email may be too short or empty.

---

## 8. Security & Privacy

The extension processes email content only when the user manually triggers analysis.

• No CRM passwords are stored
• No automatic email sending
• No permanent storage of customer email content

All communication is securely transmitted.
