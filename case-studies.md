# AI Email Intelligence for Zoho CRM — Case Studies

## Case Study 1: Improving Sales Follow-ups for a Software Services Company

### Background

A growing software services company manages customer inquiries and proposals through Zoho CRM.
Sales representatives communicate primarily through email conversations linked to Contact records.

### Challenge

The sales team faced several problems:

• Long email threads were difficult to read
• Important customer questions were sometimes missed
• Follow-up actions were not consistently tracked
• Response time to prospects was slow

Sales representatives often spent significant time reviewing past conversations before replying, which delayed communication and affected conversion rates.

### Solution

The company implemented **AI Email Intelligence for Zoho CRM** to assist sales users directly within the CRM interface.

Using the extension, the team could:

• Instantly summarize customer emails
• Extract required actions from conversations
• Automatically create follow-up tasks
• Generate professional reply drafts

### Results

After adoption:

• Sales representatives understood conversations faster
• Follow-ups were recorded more consistently
• Response time improved significantly
• Customer engagement increased

The team reduced time spent reading email threads and focused more on customer interaction.

## Case Study 2: Account Management Team Handling High Email Volume

### Background

An account management team managing multiple clients received large volumes of daily customer emails.

### Challenge

Account managers struggled to:

• Track pending customer requests
• Remember commitments made in earlier emails
• Respond quickly during busy periods

### Solution

By installing AI Email Intelligence, account managers could analyze email content directly inside Zoho CRM and generate structured summaries and suggested responses.

### Results

• Faster response to customers
• Clear tracking of requested actions
• Improved communication consistency across team members

The extension helped the team maintain service quality even during peak workload.
