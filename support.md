## 1. Contacting Support

If you need assistance with installation, configuration, or usage of the extension, please contact our support team.

**Support Email:** [support@techiesaround.com](mailto:support@techiesaround.com)

Please include the following details when contacting us:

* Your Zoho CRM Organization ID
* Your Zoho CRM data center (US / EU / IN)
* Extension version
* Description of the issue
* Screenshots (if available)

Providing these details helps us resolve your issue faster.

## 3. What We Support

We provide assistance for:

## 4. What Is Not Covered

Support does not include:

* Custom feature development
* Third-party Zoho customization
* Issues caused by other extensions
* Network or firewall restrictions in your organization
* Zoho CRM platform outages

For Zoho CRM platform problems, please contact Zoho CRM support directly.

## 6. Known Limitations

AI-generated responses depend on the quality of the input email content.
The extension may not produce meaningful output for empty or extremely short emails.
